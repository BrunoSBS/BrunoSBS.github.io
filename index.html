I'm a Postdoctoral Researcher at IACM FORTH. My research interests include topological solitons in classical field theory such as magnetic skyrmions in chiral magnets. From 2018 to 2022, I worked on my PhD at Heriot-Watt University under the supervision of Bernd Schroers. From 2022 to 2023 I was a tutor and lecturer at the African Institute of Mathematical Sciences at the Senegal centre (AIMS-Senegal). I'm currently on the committee of the Solitons at Work network.
